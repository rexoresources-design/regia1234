Section 10 · FAQ

Answer objections without losing the style.

Is this a real 3D model?

This version combines CSS 3D transforms, parallax, depth layers, animated rings, particles and floating product imagery. It can later be upgraded to a full WebGL model.

Can the same design work for other products?

Yes—perfume, gadgets, drinks, supplements, fashion items, cosmetics and luxury services can use the same cinematic structure.

Can the product image be replaced later?

Yes. Transparent PNG packshots, custom renders, generated scenes and short video loops can be inserted without rebuilding the whole page.
